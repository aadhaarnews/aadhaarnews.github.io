Supreme Court asks to link SIM cards to Aadhaar card within one year

  • Feb 8, 2017
In a bid to crackdown fraudulent activities that take place through mobile phones and sim cards, the Supreme Court of India has asked the government to place a mechanism to “verify all mobile phone subscribers and scrutinize their identity to ensure their SIM cards are not misused.” The government has been asked to link all mobile numbers to Aadhaar card within a period of one year.
