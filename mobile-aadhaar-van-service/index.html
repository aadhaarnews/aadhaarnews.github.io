Mobile Aadhaar Van Service

  • Feb 16, 2017

Vakrangee is currently trading at Rs. 308.45, up by 3.70 points or 1.21% from its previous closing of Rs. 304.75 on the BSE.

The scrip opened at Rs. 305.80 and has touched a high and low of Rs. 308.85 and Rs. 305.80 respectively. So far 8435 shares were traded on the counter.

The BSE group ‘A’ stock of face value Rs. 1 has touched a 52 week high of Rs. 315.00 on 06-Feb-2017 and a 52 week low of Rs. 160.10 on 09-Jun-2016.

Last one week high and low of the scrip stood at Rs. 309.40 and Rs. 298.20 respectively. The current market cap of the company is Rs. 16326.82 crore.

The promoters holding in the company stood at 41.60%, while institutions and non-institutions held 23.80% and 34.60% respectively.

Vakrangee along with Union Bank of India (UBI) and UIDAI has launched a fully equipped Mobile Aadhaar Van Service, to provide user-friendly facilitation of Aadhaar Enrollment, Updation and Printing services, for Senior and Physically challenged residents. Union Bank of India is a registrar on behalf of UIDAI and has engaged Vakrangee as an enrolling agency (EA) for setting up enrollment centers, undertaking collection of demographic and biometric data for UID enrollments.

Vakrangee is the unique technology driven company focused on building India’s largest network of last-mile retail touch points to deliver real-time banking, insurance, e-governance, e-commerce and ATM services to the unserved rural, semi-urban and urban markets. These retail touch points are called as ‘Vakrangee Kendra’ which acts as the ‘One-stop shop’ for availing various services and products.
